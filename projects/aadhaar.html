UX Design Project

Aadhaar Kiosk

The Aadhaar Kiosk project is a solution to the real-world problem of the Aadhaar enrolment and updation process, which was posted in Smart India Hackathon

You can view the detailed case study and SRS document on
Behance

Project Summarized

Problem Solution
Booking appointments. No more appointments, neaerest kiosk does the job.
Queues and wait times in Aadhaar centers. Multiple kiosks in government offices reduce waiting time drastically.
Intervention of third party in Aadhaar centers. Complete privacy as user enters his data in kiosk, no third party involved.
Local language issue, person living in non native place might face language barrier issues. Multilingual kiosks.
Fixed aadhaar centers, users sometimes have to travel far to get to centers. Multiple kiosks spread out in all government offices like police stations.
Aadhaar procedure sometimes require external support for non tech savvy people. Clear instructions plus 24*7 screen share support available in kiosk.
