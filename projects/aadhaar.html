Design Project

Aadhaar Kiosk

The Aadhaar Kiosk project is our solution to the real-world problem of the Aadhaar enrolment and updation process, which was posted in the Science India Hackathon

Aadhaar Kiosk - how it solves problems

Problem Solution
Booking appointments. No more appointments, neaerest kiosk does the job.
Queues and wait times in Aadhaar centers. Multiple kiosks in government offices reduce waiting time drastically.
Intervention of third party in Aadhaar centers. Complete privacy as user enters his data in kiosk, no third party involved.
Local language issue, person living in non native place might face language barrier issues. Multilingual kiosks.
Fixed aadhaar centers, users sometimes have to travel far to get to centers. Multiple kiosks spread out in all government offices like police stations.
Aadhaar procedure sometimes require external support for non tech savvy people. Clear instructions plus 24*7 screen share support available in kiosk.

1. Description

Aadhaar Kiosk is all in one machine enabling users to enroll and update without third party intervention.

2. Goal

To provide a multifucntional kiosk that has high scalability and robustness. This kiosk is aimeed for Indian population who have basic literacy and minimum tech knowledge

3. Kiosk Design

Inspired by Indian Railways, this design is to incorporate functions like iris scanning, fingerprint recognition plus scan etc. Kiosk design based on research on human anthrompometric measures
