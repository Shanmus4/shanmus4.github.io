Design Case Study

Orange Slice

Orange Slice is a recipe application that solves major problems faced by home cooks using internet to find recipes

Back Story

Being a lover of cooking myself, I almost always used youtube to follow recipes. However I faced many problems like quantity change, timing, missing ingredients etc. That's when I thought of Orange Slice

1. Description

Orange Slice is not just a typical recipe app, we have provided multiple functionalities which will make your overall kitchen life more pleasant. Built in grocery bag, partnership with supermart, brand recommendations, cooking time and ingredients adjusted to match number of servings and more!

2. Goal

To provide a simple UI for users to navigate through their favourite recipes. Making usage of recipes should also be made easy. We want the app to do all the heavy lifting. We want everything to be one click away for the user

3. Design

The app was designed keeping the users in mind. Extant research enabled me to come up with the design elements in the UI. Check out the embedded prototype below. Full project case study here
