Design Case Study

Orange Slice

Orange Slice is a recipe application that solves major problems faced by home cooks using internet to find recipes

Recipe Preparation Problems and Solutions

Problem Solution
The amount of base ingredients required for a recipe varies based on the number of servings. Automatically adjust the recipe instructions to reflect the desired number of servings.
It can be difficult to obtain the necessary ingredients for a recipe. Incorporate a built-in grocery list feature and partner with a supermarket to streamline the process of obtaining ingredients.
The cooking time for a recipe can vary depending on the number of servings. Automatically adjust the recipe instructions to reflect the desired number of servings.
Lengthy recipe videos can be overwhelming. Use concise recipe videos with highlighted key steps.
The nutritional information for a recipe is not readily available. Make the nutritional information easily accessible and categorize recipes based on nutritional value.

1. Description

Orange Slice is an all in one recipe app allowing users to find and follow recipes very easily

2. Goal

To provide an application that even a non tech savvy person would find easy to use
